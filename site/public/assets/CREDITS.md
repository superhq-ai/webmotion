# Asset credits

Everything the site ships is either public domain or made for this repo. None of
it carries an attribution requirement; provenance is recorded here as good
practice, and the hero film puts the same details on screen as wall labels.

## Artwork

Sourced through [Wikimedia Commons](https://commons.wikimedia.org/), all public
domain. Regenerate with `python3 scripts/make-art-plates.py`.

- `david.glb` - 3D scan of a plaster cast of the head of Michelangelo's *David*,
  inventory KAS2232,
  [Statens Museum for Kunst](https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Michelangelo_Buonarroti,_Hoved_fra_statuen_af_David,_,_KAS2232,_Statens_Museum_for_Kunst,_3D_model.stl).
  Reduced from 1,083,370 to 60,000 triangles in Blender and Draco-compressed:
  54 MB STL to 172 KB glTF.
- `art/mona.png` - Leonardo da Vinci, *La Gioconda*, c. 1503, Musee du Louvre
  ([C2RMF scan](https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Mona_Lisa,_by_Leonardo_da_Vinci,_from_C2RMF_retouched.jpg)).
- `art/vitruvian.png` - Leonardo da Vinci, *Le proporzioni del corpo umano*,
  c. 1490, Gallerie dell'Accademia
  ([photograph by Luc Viatour](https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Da_Vinci_Vitruve_Luc_Viatour.jpg)).
- `art/ermine.png` - Leonardo da Vinci, *Dama con l'ermellino*, c. 1490,
  Czartoryski Museum
  ([Commons](https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Leonardo_da_Vinci_-_Lady_with_an_Ermine.jpg)).
